Chibwabwa Recipe

Chipampila is a combination of Kidney leaves and beans.

It is mainly served with Nsima

Preparation and cooking time average (30 minutes - 2 hours). Quick (30 minutes or less).

INGREDIENTS

  • 200 gramme of beans
  • 500 gramme of kidney (leaves)
  • 1 cup(s) of groundnuts
  • seasonings
